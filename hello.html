Plants: Green, Quiet, Essential

A tiny guide to types and simple care tips.

Plants bring life to rooms, clean the air a bit, and soothe the mind. Below are a few common types and very simple care tips you can try at home.

Succulents

Low-water lovers that store moisture in their leaves. Give bright light and sparse watering.

Ferns

Enjoy humidity and indirect light. Keep soil lightly moist and mist occasionally.

Herbs

Basil, mint, and rosemary thrive on sunny windowsills and regular harvesting.

Fiddle Leaf Fig

Showy indoor tree needing bright, indirect light and moderate watering.
